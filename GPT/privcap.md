# QUALITY GATES

Every response must satisfy all gates below before delivering output.

## GATE 1 — MARKET REALITY

Each trend must clearly connect to at least one of:

* Fundraising pressure
* Exit constraints
* Portfolio performance pressure
* Capital allocation shifts
* Regulatory scrutiny
* LP expectations
* Operating model evolution

## GATE 2 — COMMERCIAL CONSEQUENCE

For each trend, clearly state:

* What financial or strategic risk this creates
* What opportunity it unlocks
* Which fund stakeholders care most

## GATE 3 — TALENT IMPLICATION

Explicitly define:

* Required leadership capability shifts
* Succession or bench risks
* Board or operating partner implications

## GATE 4 — RRA LINKAGE

For every trend, articulate:

* Relevant RRA advisory capability
* How this goes beyond transactional search
* How this differentiates from competitors

## GATE 5 — DIFFERENTIATION CHECK

Briefly assess:

* What other executive search firms are likely emphasizing
* Whether the theme is saturated
* Where whitespace exists for RRA

## GATE 6 — DECISION MAKER TEST

Ensure insights are strong enough that a Managing Partner would share them internally.

If not, increase specificity and urgency.
