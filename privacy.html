Privacy Policy for Refund Checker

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Overview

Refund Checker is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Information Collection

We do not collect any user data.

This includes (but is not limited to):

  • Personal information (name, email address, etc.)
  • Browsing history
  • Purchase history (outside of real-time processing)
  • Financial information
  • Authentication data

How the Extension Works

Refund Checker operates entirely within your browser. Any data accessed (such as order details on supported websites) is processed locally and is never sent to external servers.

Data Storage

  • All data (such as settings or tracked items) is stored locally on your device using Chrome’s storage APIs.
  • You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time by removing the extension or clearing browser storage.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

User Control

  • You can enable or disable the extension at any time
  • You can uninstall the extension to remove all stored data
  • You can clear browser storage to delete any saved preferences

Security

Because no data is transmitted or stored externally, your information remains private and secure within your own browser.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.
